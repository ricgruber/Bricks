Bill Wilson Founder/Co-Owner

Bill and his loving wife Donna opened the very first Bricks in 2006. The two continue to burn wood everyday, giving loyal Bricks customers the very best in wood-fired cuisine. In 2012, Bill and Donna happily partnered with Sam and Patricia. As a team, they have created a newfound excitement for the future of Bricks and its continued growth. It is their dream for everyone to experience all the goodness that comes with an authentic brick oven.

Sam Vlahos Co-Owner

Sam has spent many valuable years learning everything about the restaurant business under the watch of his father, John Vlahos, a well known figure in the industry. With a degree in Business Management from Elmhurst College, and a focus on entrepreneurship, Sam has broken into the business at a young age. He is ready to bring a fresh approach to the Bricks concept and is excited to expand and evolve Bricks Wood Fired Pizza and Cafe.

Patricia Vlahos-Segretto Co-Owner

Patricia has grown up in the restaurant industry, working alongside restauranteur-father, John Vlahos. Her passion for food and entrepreneurship has developed from her experiences working in successful restaurants, as well as her culinary education from Le Cordon Bleu. She is thrilled to bring her broad knowledge and expertise to Bricks, working alongside Bill and brother Sam to deliver an unforgettable experience to hungry patrons.
